Game info

Type: Simple - Decks: 1 deck (52 cards) - Redeals: None

  • Game time: Quick
  • Difficulty level: Easy
  • Skill level: Mostly chance
  • Chance of winning: Low (about 1 in 20)

Object of the game: Move all the cards to the foundation.

Rules

Foundations (One pile, next to the stock)

  • There is only one foundation, which is dealt face up at the start of the game.
  • Build up or down, regardless of suit. For example, on a 10 you can place either a 9 or a Jack .
  • Ranking of cards is continuous: build Kings after Aces or Aces after Kings when necessary.

Tableau (Seven columns of five cards each)

  • No building on the tableau.
  • The top card of each pile is available for play to the foundation.

Stock (Sixteen cards)

  • When there are no more possible plays, turn cards up, one at a time, to the foundation by clicking.
  • There is no redeal.

Strategy

  • When you can play more than one card to the foundation, look at which cards will be exposed. Try to play the card that will maximize the number of cards you can remove.
  • Remembering what cards have been played will prove useful.
