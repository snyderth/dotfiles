Game info

Type: Adding - Decks: 1 deck (52 cards) - Redeals: 2

  • Game time: Medium
  • Difficulty level: Easy
  • Skill level: Mostly chance
  • Chance of winning: Medium (about 1 in 6 games)

Object of the game: Discard all the cards in the Pyramid (not the entire stock) in pairs totaling thirteen.

Rules

Tableau (Twenty-eight cards in the form of a pyramid)

  • Discard any pair of available cards, regardless of suit, which total value is equal to 13.
  • Available cards are:
    • Two exposed (not covered by any other card) pyramid cards;
    • The stock card and an exposed pyramid card;
    • The top waste card and an exposed pyramid card;
    • The stock card and top waste card.
  • The valid pairs are:
    • A and Q
    • 2 and J
    • 3 and 10
    • 4 and 9
    • 5 and 8
    • 6 and 7
  • Jacks are worth 11. Queens are worth 12.
  • Kings are worth 13 and are discarded singularly. To discard a King, simply click on it.

Stock (Top left)

  • Turn up one card at a time, from the stock to the waste, by clicking.
  • Two redeals are permitted (three deals in all).
Waste (One pile to the right of the stock)
  • The top card is available for play.

Strategy

  • Be careful not to block plays by leaving cards at the top of the pyramid that can be matched.
